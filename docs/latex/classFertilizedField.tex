\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A concrete decorator for \mbox{\hyperlink{classCropField}{Crop\+Field}} that adds fertilization functionality. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classFertilizedField}{Fertilized\+Field}} class enhances a \mbox{\hyperlink{classCropField}{Crop\+Field}} by increasing its production capacity and providing additional methods for managing trucks, soil states, and harvesting.
