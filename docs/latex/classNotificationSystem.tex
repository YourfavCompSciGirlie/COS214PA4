\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Implements the Subject role in the Observer design pattern. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classNotificationSystem}{Notification\+System}} class maintains a list of observers (trucks) and notifies them of important events such as fertilizer requirements or when storage capacity is reached.
